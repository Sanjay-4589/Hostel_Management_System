The following facilities are being offered in post-matric Hostel.

  1. Food will be given to each student for a period of 10 months at a cost of Rs.1600/- per month.
  2. Free accommodation.
  3. Contigency expenses for each hostel Rs.1,000/- per month for a period of 10 months will be covered.
  4. For cooking vessel maintenance Rs.600/- for thehostel with sanction number 50 and Rs.1000/- for the hostel having more than 50 sanction numbers will be covered per year.
  5. Rs. 1250 / - per annum for each student hostels for cleaning the toilets expenses will be covered.
  6. Supply of 2 daily newspapers for each hostel at actual cost (One Kannada and one English language newspaper).
  7. Payment of rent at the rate approved by the Administrative Department after the rent of the buildings is fixed by the Department of Public Works.

    1. For library of postgraduate / professional students Rs.1.25 lakhsfor the first time, thereafter Rs.30,000/- per annum.
    2. For other students hostel Rs.1.00 laksh for the first time, thereafter Rs.20,000/- per annum.

  9. Sports equipment once a year, per hostel at actual rate.

Allotments of Hostels rooms to interns. Hostels rooms are allotted to external summer intern and also to the interns during winter vacation provided they are working with a faculty member from IIT Bombay. The interns are required to complete the formalities such as registration, payment of internship fees etc. with the academic office and thereafter visit HCU (only during working hours) for allotment of accommodation.
